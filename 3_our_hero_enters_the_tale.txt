A handsome man appears! He is so handsome that women and men faint when he walks by.
He always have a bare upperbody, even in winter to show off his pecs and his name is Niclas. 
He is the hero of this tale. 

Niclas has a sidekick, Jeff. 
Jeff is the village idiot, and worships his own deity, Nicolaus the Cagekeeper.
For Jeff, Nicolaus is the truth, Nicolaus is the way...Nicolaus is life!He desire him! 
Whenever Niclas' decisions conflict with Nicolaus', Jeff gets upset and pulls his hair 
and sometimes a tooth or nostrils.
